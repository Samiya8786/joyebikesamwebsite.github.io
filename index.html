  • About Joy E Bike Monster

Joy e-bike Monster is an electric commuter bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,00,875 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 2 colours. Joy e-bike Monster generates 250 W power from its motor. Joy e-bike Monster comes up with both front and rear disc brakes. Joy e-bike is an electric two-wheeler brand that is owned by Wardwizard Innovations & Mobility Limited and it has eight offerings in its portfolio. The Monster, in particular, gets a motorcycle-like styling that is closely similar to the Honda Grom 125 mini-bike. With a low seat height and extremely light weight of 75kgs, the Monster should be easily accessible to all kind of riders. Although small, it comes equipped with some premium components like upside down forks up front, 14-inch alloy wheels and tubeless tyres. The Monster runs on a 250 watt brushless DC hub motor that is powered by a 72V/32Ah lithium-ion battery. While the battery range is impressive at 75kms, the top speed is as low as 25kmph. The battery takes around 4.5 hours to fully top up. It is available in two colour options black and red.
